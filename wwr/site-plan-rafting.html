Overview

Purpose

Our purpose here at Dry Oar Rafting is to provide you with the best white water rafting experience in Colorado and beyond. We strive for safety on the Colorado and Fork rivers, while still making your experience on them fun and invigorating.

Audience

We provide services for both experienced rafters and those who have never before touched a paddle. There are also mild to strong rafting trips for families of different ages and skill levels. So for any group or individual searching for an outdoor adventure, we've got you covered.
